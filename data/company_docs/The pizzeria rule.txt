The pizzeria DELHI – OPERATING RULES AND POLICIES

BASIC INFO
Restaurant name: Trattoria Delhi
Cuisine: Italian
City: New Delhi
Neighbourhood: Greater Kailash II

OPENING HOURS
- Monday to Thursday: 11:00 AM – 11:00 PM
- Friday and Saturday: 11:00 AM – 12:00 Midnight
- Sunday: 11:00 AM – 11:00 PM

Last order times:
- Dine-in and pickup: 30 minutes before closing time
- Delivery: 45 minutes before closing time

PICKUP VS DELIVERY

Pickup:
- Available every day during opening hours.
- Orders can be scheduled for a specific time within the same day.
- Typical preparation time: 20–30 minutes depending on order size.

Delivery:
- Delivery available within selected areas of South Delhi only.
- Primary delivery neighbourhoods:
  - Greater Kailash I and II
  - Kailash Colony
  - Lajpat Nagar
  - East of Kailash
- If the user’s address is outside these areas, inform them that delivery is not available and suggest pickup.

DELIVERY FEES AND MINIMUM ORDER

Minimum order for delivery:
- ₹600 (food total, before taxes and delivery fee).

Delivery fee:
- Within 3 km radius: ₹79
- Between 3 km and 6 km: ₹129
- Beyond 6 km: delivery is not available.

Free delivery:
- Orders above ₹1500 get free delivery within the service area.

ESTIMATED DELIVERY TIMES
- Typical delivery time: 35–55 minutes depending on distance and order size.
- During peak hours (8–10 PM) delivery may take up to 60–75 minutes.

ORDER CONSTRAINTS

- Maximum pizzas per single order via phone/voice assistant: 8.
- Large group/catering orders (more than 8 pizzas) should be requested at least 4 hours in advance.
- Custom or off-menu items are not allowed; the assistant should only use items from the menu context.
- Half-and-half pizzas are not supported at this time.

PAYMENT METHODS

Accepted payment methods:
- Cash on delivery (COD) for delivery orders.
- UPI (via QR code or link).
- Major credit and debit cards for both pickup and delivery.
- For voice orders, if user selects prepaid, payment link or instructions are sent by SMS/WhatsApp (handled offline by staff; the assistant should mention that payment details will be shared separately).

TAXES AND CHARGES

- Prices shown in the menu are base prices and do not include taxes.
- Applicable GST will be added to the final bill.
- Service charge is not automatically added; any tip is optional.

ALLERGY AND DIETARY INFORMATION

- Many items contain dairy and gluten.
- Pizzas can be made with gluten-free base (limited options; see menu).
- We cannot guarantee a completely nut-free or gluten-free kitchen.
- The assistant should recommend that customers with severe allergies consult the restaurant directly.

GENERAL GUIDELINES FOR THE ASSISTANT

- Always be polite, concise, and friendly.
- If unsure whether an item or option exists, say you are not sure and avoid making it up.
- Use the opening hours, delivery areas, and minimum order rules defined in this document.
- If a customer wants something outside the rules (e.g., delivery outside zone), gently suggest pickup as an alternative.
